How It Works

Understanding the core mechanic of HardcoreCiv

One Life Per Hour

Die once and you're out for 1 hour. This isn't permadeath—it's a strategic timeout that keeps the stakes high and the gameplay intense. Every decision matters.

100% Fair Play

Completely free to play. No pay-to-win. No advantages for money. Every player gets the exact same tools and opportunities. Pure skill and strategy decide who thrives.

Community Built

Voice chat, teleports, and home commands keep players connected. Work together, build bases, and survive as a team. The server is only as strong as its community.

Server Features

Tools designed to enhance your hardcore survival experience

Proximity Voice Chat

Talk to nearby players in real-time. Coordinate raids, warn teammates of danger, and celebrate victories together without ever leaving the game.

Teleport System

Send TPA requests to friends or use RTP to discover new lands. Meet up instantly after long journeys. Brings teammates together safely across the map.

Home Waypoint

Set your personal home base and teleport back anytime. Your safe hub, your spawn point, your fortress. Never lose your way back to safety.
